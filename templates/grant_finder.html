What Is Grant Finder?

Grant Finder is a funding intelligence and grant-readiness platform within the ChapNetAI ecosystem.

It was created to help organizations identify funding opportunities, organize grant efforts, and improve preparation for competitive grant programs.

Who It Helps

Nonprofit organizations
Community initiatives
Recovery programs
Workforce development organizations
Mission-driven projects seeking funding

Mission

Grant Finder exists to reduce the complexity of funding discovery and grant preparation.

The goal is to help organizations spend less time searching and more time building programs that create measurable community impact.

Development Status

Grant Finder is currently a private development system within the ChapNetAI ecosystem.

Public visitors can learn about the mission through the Community Hub while operational workflows, grant intelligence tools, and development environments remain private.
