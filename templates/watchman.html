What Is Watchman?

Watchman is the oversight, validation, and ecosystem intelligence platform within ChapNetAI.

It was designed to help monitor system health, track progress, support decision-making, and provide visibility across multiple initiatives and projects.

Core Responsibilities

Ecosystem monitoring
Project oversight
Progress tracking
Operational intelligence
Validation and accountability

Mission

Watchman exists to provide visibility, coordination, and oversight across the ChapNetAI ecosystem.

Its purpose is to help ensure that projects remain aligned with mission objectives, maintain accountability, and support informed decision-making.

Development Status

Watchman is currently a private development system.

Public visitors can learn about the mission through the Community Hub while operational dashboards, intelligence workflows, and ecosystem management tools remain private.
