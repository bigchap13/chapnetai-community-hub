What Is Walker County's Digital Community Square?

Walker County's Digital Community Square is a local-first platform for residents, businesses, organizations, events, opportunities, resources, and community updates.

It is designed to help residents, businesses, organizations, community leaders, and local initiatives connect, communicate, and share information that strengthens the community.

Mission

Walker County's Digital Community Square exists to strengthen local connections and create a digital gathering place where people, organizations, businesses, and opportunities can be discovered and shared.

The long-term vision is to help communities become more connected, informed, engaged, and supportive of one another.

Development Status

Walker County's Digital Community Square is currently a private development system within the ChapNetAI ecosystem.

Public visitors can learn about the vision through the Community Hub while platform development continues.
