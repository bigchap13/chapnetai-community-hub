What Is Local Loop?

Local Loop is Walker County's Digital Community Square.

It is designed to help residents, businesses, organizations, community leaders, and local initiatives connect, communicate, and share information that strengthens the community.

What Can Be Shared?

Community Updates
Business Announcements
Local Events
Workforce Opportunities
Volunteer Opportunities
Community Resources
Organization Updates
Neighborhood Conversations

Mission

Local Loop exists to strengthen local connections and create a digital gathering place where people, organizations, businesses, and opportunities can be discovered and shared.

The long-term vision is to help communities become more connected, informed, engaged, and supportive of one another.

Development Status

Local Loop is currently a private development system within the ChapNetAI ecosystem.

Public visitors can learn about the vision through the Community Hub while platform development continues.
